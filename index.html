Here's time line of the life of Billy Holiday:

  • 1915 - Born to a 19 year-old single mother
  • 1933 - Discovered by record producer John Hammond
  • 1935 - Hammond signed her to Brunswick Records with pianist Teddy Wilson
  • 1937 - Joined the Count Basle Orchestra
  • 1941 - Met & married playboy James Monroe
  • 1944 - Signed with Decca Records
  • 1947 - Arrested & convicted for narcotics possession, pleaded guilty & sentenced to a year and a day
  • 1959 - Died in a NYC hospital at age 44
